Non-Semantic Tags in HTML

Non-semantic are those who do not decribe thier meaning and propose to browser and developer they are just a genric (general) container for content or tags itself

Some General Non-Semantic Tgs

  • div (used as a genric container)
  • span (used for inline genric container)
  • b (bold the text)
  • ul (make unorder list)
  • ol (make order list)
  • hr (for horizental line)
  • i (for italic text)
  • font f(or change the properties and styling of fonts)
  • center (used to center the content)
  • small (make text smaller)
  • big (make text bigger)
